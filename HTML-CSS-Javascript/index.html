More Or Less

How to play

The computer draws a number according to the interval of your level.
He asks you to guess the number. You enter a number.
The computer compares the number you entered with the mystery number it drew.
You are told that the number is higher or lower than the one you entered.
And it tells you if the number is mystery is higher or lower.
And so on, until you find the mystery number.

New Feature:A new background is available, go to the setting menu !
